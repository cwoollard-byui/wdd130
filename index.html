England

England is a country which is part of the United Kingdom and is on the island of Great Britain. It has land borders with Scotland and Wales and is separated from Ireland by the Irish Sea. The largest city and capital of both England and the United Kingdom is London, the Royal Family reside there of which King Charles III is the current monarch. In the mid-19th century (ca. 1850) there were more members the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in England than there were in the United States.
