Our Rafting Trips

From gentle scenic floats to heart-pounding whitewater challenges, Wild Water Rapids has an adventure for everyone. All trips include expert guides, safety equipment, and unforgettable memories.

Half Day Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, our Half Day Adventure takes you through Class II and III rapids on a scenic stretch of river. Enjoy stunning canyon views while learning the basics of whitewater rafting from our friendly, experienced guides. No prior experience needed.

Full Day Expedition

Our most popular trip. Spend a full day on the river tackling Class III and IV rapids, with a riverside lunch included. This expedition covers over 15 miles of river and features thrilling rapids, calm pools for swimming, and incredible scenery throughout the journey.

Extreme Challenge

For seasoned adventurers seeking the ultimate adrenaline rush. Our Extreme Challenge takes on Class IV and V rapids with steep drops, powerful currents, and nonstop action. Prior rafting experience is required. This two-day trip includes camping along the riverbank.
